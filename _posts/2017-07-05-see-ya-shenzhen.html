We left Friday morning to fly to Shanghai. I meet many cool and interesting people in Shanghai. I will miss them.
Interestingly, I became friends with a really good photographer, and he took lots of cool pictures of me and Tiago.
Also, I have some more fun pictures from the city.
We also went and stole some Lychee fruit off the trees around the SusTech campus. Apparently, there is a fine if you get caught and you risk running into nasty snakes. However, I think we went after they had already harvested most of the trees, so I felt justified to pick a few kilos of fruit.
The photographer also took me to the top an apartment to see the city.
